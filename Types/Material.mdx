## Properties
#### `glow: float`
Specifies the amount of glow the material has

#### `texture: int`
Specifies the amount of detail the material has

#### `roughness: float`
Specifies how rough the material is

#### `recieve_shadows: bool`
Determines whether or not the material should be receptive to shadows
